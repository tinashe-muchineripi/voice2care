At Voice2Care we focus on providing home based quality care to service users. Our main priority is to ensure you receive the best care possible.

Personal Care

Live-In Care

Palliative Care

Our palliative carers help you while you are comfortable in your home, sorrounded by your family and friends.

How long does it take to get everything arranged?

It depends on your needs. If your family's care needs are urgent, we can put everything in place in as soon as 48 hours.

Can the carer take my loved one out?

Certainly one of the main advantages of live-in care is that your elderly loved one can continue to live as normally as possible. This can mean going for walks, going to the park, or simply going to the shops.

Can I change carers?

You don't need to worry if you don't feel the carer we selected for you is right. We'll be able to find another match for you quickly. Just contact us to let us know.
