About Us

Voice2Care is a domiciliary care provider that provides home based quality care to service users. Our services are regulated by the Care Quality Commission in line with the Health and Social Care Act 2008.

Values

  • Respect
  • Independence
  • Equality
  • Competence
  • Commitment

Voice2Care Ltd strives to offer safe and quality home care services to individuals and families in and around Leeds Council and surrounding areas.

It is our goal to employ competent, caring, and well-trained individuals who are responsive to the needs of our clients, their families, and the communities we serve. Each staff member will meet the educational and training requirements for the services they provide.

We encourage and support continued education of each individual staff. In turn, our company will provide staff with competitive compensation, an inviting work environment, and knowledgeable, trustworthy management and direction.
