Services

Personal Care

Our personal care services include all personal daily tasks you may need assistance with, including bathing, showering, eating. In addition, they can manage continence issues and general laundry at your own convenience, ranging from creams and lotions to dressing.

Live-In Care

The support of a live-in carer can dramatically improve your quality of life. A live-in carer is not limited just to handling personal care, mobility issues, or administering medicine; they can provide much, much more.

Palliative Care

The problems of loneliness in the elderly are well known. Companionship care is essentially the same as live-in care, but the emphasis is on providing companionship rather than high levels of personal care.

Waking Nights

Care professionals are on duty for at least eight hours each night. This is the best option if you or a loved one wake several times in the night perhaps being disoriented or needing to use the bathroom.
